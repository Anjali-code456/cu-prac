Bachelor of Arts in Journalism & Mass Communication

Program Overview

The Online BA in Journalism and Mass Communication is a 3 year undergraduate Programme that covers the broad discipline of media studies. It involves a theoretical understanding of the history, content and cultural impact of all types of media along with an applied learning focus in areas such as print, radio, television and new media, and their specific tools and techniques. It also informs students about various technology, equipment and software involved in mass media. The duration of the course is three years and is divided into six equal semesters.

The Online BA in JMC offers an insight to mass communication theories- history/models, Print Journalism, Advertising, Photography, Television and Radio Production and Writing, Convergent Journalism, Social Media, Public Relations, etc.

  • UGC-entitled degree programme
  • Built-in Internship/Job opportunity with access to job portal with 300+ hiring partners
  • Earn a degree from NAAC A+ accredited university
  • Cost-effective with EMI options
Minimum Eligibility

10+2 with at least 50% marks in aggregate or its equivalent examination in any stream conducted by a recognized Board / University / Council in the aggregate OR 45% marks in 10+2 in any stream with at least 55% in English as the subject.
